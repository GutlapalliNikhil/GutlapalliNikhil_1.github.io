World Robot Summit - Japan 2020 (Postponed)

We team, Paricharya from the Humanitarian Technology (HuT) Labs, Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, School of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri campus, won 1 Million Japanese Yen (Approx. 15 lakhs INR) towards participating in 2020 World Robot Summit to be held in Japan. Of 119 professional teams that applied from all over the world, Amrita's two teams are the only teams from India to qualify for the finals. In October 2020, the team "Paricharya" will take part in the 'Future Convenience Store Challenge' while the team "Vijayanta" in the 'Standard Disaster Robotic Challenge'. They will compete with top-class professional teams from all over the world.

For more details, check out https://amrita.edu/news/amrita-teams-win-225-million-japanese-yen-towards-world-robot-summit-2020

Robocup@German HomeLegue 2019

The evaluation for this competition is based on the tasks we have done with our Autonomous Service Robot. We are the first team from India to participate in Home league of Robocup, Germany.

For more details, check out https://www.amrita.edu/research/project/service-robot.
