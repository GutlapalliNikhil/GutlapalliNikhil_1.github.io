Integration of Vision based Robot Manipulation using ROS for Assistive Applications

G Nikhil Chowdary, R. K. Megalingam, R. V. Rohith Raj, T. Akhil, A. Masetti and V. S. Naick. 2020 Second International Conference on Inventive Research in Computing Applications (ICIRCA), Coimbatore, India, 2020, pp. 163-169, doi: 10.1109/ICIRCA48905.2020.9183013.

This paper mainly focuses on integration of kinova robotic arm with depth camera. We have integrated the system such that, when the required object is provided to the system, the camera detects the objects and send the respective coordinate system for the arm to pick the object.

For paper, please refer https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/9183013

Assessment of Water Quality Index and Study of the Impact of Pollution on the Rivers of Kerala (Yet to be published)

Dr. Jyothi SN, Nikhil Chowdary G, Akhil Masetti, Akhil Tammana, Manaswini Motheram, Rohith Raj RV and Gevargis Muramthookil Thomas. Proceedings of the International Conference on Nanoelectronics, Nanophotonics, Nanomaterials, Nanobioscience & Nanotechnology (5NANO2020) held on 23rd & 24th April 2020.

It is difficult for a layman to understand the significance of the various parameters being monitored by the Pollution Control Board. Water Quality Index, which encompasses the impact of these physical and chemical parameters on the quality of a water sample, can be used as an effective tool to convert convoluted parameters into a more comprehensible form. This study develops a generalized and reliable C language code to quickly compute the Water Quality Index for different water bodies. The Water Quality Index is calculated, using the code, for some of the rivers in key geographic locations of Kerala.

Human Robot Interaction on Navigation platform using Robot Operating System

G Nikhil Chowdary, R. K. Megalingam, M. Manaswini, J. Yannam and V. S. Naick, 2020 Fourth International Conference on Inventive Systems and Control (ICISC), Coimbatore, India, 2020, pp. 898-905, doi: 10.1109/ICISC47916.2020.9171065.

This paper focuses on integration of navigation with speech over ROS. With the help of this system, the robot can be operated through voice commands instead of teleop commands. This whole system was implemented in our service robot.

For more details, check out https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/9171065.

Design and Implementation of an Arena for Testing and Evaluating Quadcopter.

G Nikhil Chowdary, R. K. Megalingam, R. V. R. Raj, A. Masetti, T. Akhil, and V. S. Naick. 2018 4th International Conference on Computing Communication and Automation (ICCCA), Greater Noida, India, 2018, pp. 1-7, doi: 10.1109/CCAA.2018.8777578.

This paper includes designing of autonomous quadcopter and testing in different senarios. Testing the quadcopter in such different paths and conditions will give an idea about how to judge the performance and the stability of the quadcopter in different conditions for any application purpose and how the entire system responds to those conditions.

For more details, check out https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/8777578
